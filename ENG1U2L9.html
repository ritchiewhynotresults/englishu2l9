Goblet:

(n) Bowl-shaped drinking vessel without handles; a drinking pot made of glass with a foot and a stem; often used to drink wine and champagne

Marimba:

(n) Musical instrument; a xylophone of Southern Africa and Central America with resonators under each bar

Nimbus:

(n) Dark, rain-bearing cloud; a shining vapor, cloud, or atmosphere around a deity when on earth; a cloud or atmosphere around a person or thing (such as in romance); a signal (as a circle) of brilliant light or glory about the head of a drawn or sculptured deity, saint, or sovereign; a rain cloud; halo

Petunias:

(n) Any of the genus Petunia of tropical South American herbs of the nightshade family with flowers having funnel-shaped corollas
